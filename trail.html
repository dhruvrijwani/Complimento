December 2024 Strategy

Revenue Focus
Maximizing returns on spend will be the top priority, with a projected revenue target of ₹7,47,302. We'll closely monitor and scale campaigns showing strong potential.
ROAS Target
Maintaining a minimum ROAS threshold of 2.00X. Aggressive scaling of high-performing campaigns to maximize revenue without compromising efficiency.
Creative Strategy
Developing more creatives similar to top performers to drive better results. Heavy promotion of wedding collection using latest shoot images and videos for the festive and wedding season.
Product Consistency
Expanding wedding collection testing while maintaining better inventory stability for long-term consistency without availability issues.
Campaign Flexibility
Maintaining flexibility to rapidly scale campaigns demonstrating strong performance metrics and positive trends.
New Execution
Implementing Engage 360 to improve efficiency, reduce RTOs, and convert COD orders to prepaid. Utilizing messaging for abandoned cart retargeting to minimize drop-offs.
